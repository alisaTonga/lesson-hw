Description

The Arctic fox has a round body shape with short legs and a short, fluffy tail. Its fur is its most distinctive feature. In summer, it has thinner brown, dark brown, or yellow fur. However, during the winter, its fur becomes thick and white, providing excellent camouflage in snowy landscapes 1.
